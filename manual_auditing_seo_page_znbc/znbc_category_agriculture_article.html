TOBACCO MARKET OPENS WEDNESDAY

Joshua Jere
April 19, 2019
0
876
0
0

The 2019 Tobacco marketing season is officially set to start on Wednesday, April 24th, 2019.

Agriculture Minister Michael Katambo said that all approved tobacco sales floors will open effective April 24th, 2019.

Mr Katambo has explained that the challenges the Tobacco industry has been facing have been resolved by industry players with guidance from the Ministry of Agriculture.

He said the key challenge was the enactment and implementation of Statutory Instruments Number 67, 84 and 85 of 2018, which has been addressed through the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) that will guide how the marketing of Tobacco in the 2018/2019 market
ng season will operate.

Mr Katambo has urged all players in the tobacco industry to abide by MoU to ensure a smooth marketing season.

He has also disclosed that the 2018/2019 Tobacco production volumes are expected to drop to 24 Million Kilogrammes, from 31 Million Kilograms in the 2017/2018 season.

Mr Katambo said a robust integrated registration system which is on trial has captured 19, 500 registered tobacco growers for the 2018/2019 season out of who 20 percent are self-sponsored and non-contracted growers without an assured market.

He has appealed to the licensed buyers to absorb the non-contracted self-sponsored crop alongside the contracted sponsored crop.
